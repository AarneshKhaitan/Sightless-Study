# UI Copy (spoken text)

## Reading mode prompt (after chunk)
“Say Continue, Repeat, Question, Summarize, Where am I, or Help.”

## Help
“You can say Continue, Repeat, Question, Summarize this page, Where am I, Go back, or Stop.”

## Formula mode entry
“A formula is here. Say Symbols, Example, Intuition, Repeat, or Continue.”

## Visual mode entry
“A visual is here. I will describe it. Say Start exploring when ready.”

## Explore guidance (first time)
“Move your pointer around the canvas. Say What is here to hear details about your current position.”
“Say Mark this to bookmark a point.”
“Say Guide me to minimum or peak for directions.”

## Explore completion
“Say I’m done to hear what you explored, or Continue to return to the lesson.”
